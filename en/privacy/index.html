Privacy Notice

Note: The full legally reviewed privacy notice will be finalised by attorney Ms Kirsch before the public launch and integrated here. Until then, the following provisional summary describes our privacy approach.

What we do not collect

  • No names, email addresses or phone numbers
  • No advertising or tracking services
  • No third-party analytics providers
  • No cookies beyond technically necessary ones
  • No payment data (handled by Apple or Google)

What we do collect

  • Anonymous user identifier (random value generated by Firebase)
  • Subscription status (true or false) and expiry date
  • Your self-entered collection data, stored in your own area on Firebase (Google Cloud, EU region)
  • Uploaded photos of your cigars and rums (encrypted in transit, in your own storage area)

Legal basis

Processing is based on your consent at first use (Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR) and on contractual necessity (Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR) for the Premium features.

Data controller

CEDRUMO Digital Ventures GmbH i.G.
Contact: info@cedrumo.com
