Premium membership

Premium is for the moment when you don't want to search — but simply want to enjoy the right cigar. From your own collection, matched to time, mood, strength and rum.

What Premium unlocks

  • Evening Planner with eight weighted selection criteria
  • Rum collection with pairing recommendations from your own stock
  • Luxury Dashboard with value, rating, favourites and restock focus
  • Cloud sync across multiple devices
  • Extended statistics and analysis charts

Pricing

Monthly: EUR 2.99
Yearly: EUR 29.90 (equivalent to EUR 2.49 per month)

7-day free trial

Every new Premium user gets the first seven days free. After the trial period the subscription renews automatically: the monthly plan at EUR 2.99 per month, the annual plan at EUR 29.90 per year, in each case unless it is cancelled in good time before the trial period ends. You can cancel anytime in your Apple ID or Google account settings, with no friction.

Payment

Payment is handled exclusively through the official Apple and Google app stores. CEDRUMO never sees payment data. Refunds and family sharing run through Apple or Google.

Note: Final store-side pricing is displayed in each respective store after activation of the subscription model.
