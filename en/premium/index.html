Premium membership

CEDRUMO Premium activates the features that turn a good app into a truly cultivated companion.

What Premium unlocks

  • Evening Planner with eight weighted selection criteria
  • Rum collection with pairing recommendations from your own stock
  • Luxury Dashboard with value, rating, favourites and restock focus
  • Cloud sync across multiple devices
  • Extended statistics and analysis charts

Pricing

Monthly: EUR 2.99
Yearly: EUR 29.90 (equivalent to EUR 2.49 per month)

7-day free trial

Every new Premium user gets the first seven days free. No automatic conversion without confirmation. Cancel anytime in your Apple ID or Google account settings, with no friction.

Payment

Payment is handled exclusively through the official Apple and Google app stores. CEDRUMO never sees payment data. Refunds and family sharing run through Apple or Google.

Note: Final store-side pricing is displayed in each respective store after activation of the subscription model. The values above are the intended baseline.
