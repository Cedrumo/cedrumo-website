Features

Collection

Record every cigar with manufacturer, country, format, wrapper, binder, filler, strength, smoking time and personal rating. Photos, aromas, blend notes and personal annotations complete each entry.

Humidor

Assign cigars to up to three preset humidors or define your own locations. See value per humidor and total at a glance. Restock indicators warn when stock becomes critical.

Evening Planner (Premium)

Choose occasion (Relaxation, Social, Celebration, Evening, Daytime), available time, strength and rum style. Eight weighted criteria determine the recommendation from your collection.

Rum Pairing (Premium)

Track your rum collection with category (Light, Medium, Heavy, Sweet), aromas and distillery. The Evening Planner suggests a matching rum from your own collection for each cigar.

Luxury Dashboard (Premium)

Collection value, average rating, top favourites and restock focus. See immediately which cigars to restock and which highlights define your collection.

Privacy

Anonymous sign-in, no advertising, no tracking, no third-party analytics. Your collection stays entirely in your hands.
