Specialist retail meets aficionado

Retailer Area

At the right moment. In the right setting. With respect for advice and enjoyment culture.

CEDRUMO is a premium app for aficionados who want to organise their humidor, document moments of enjoyment, and make more deliberate decisions about their personal cigar culture. Alongside that, a curated orientation to selected specialist retailers is taking shape. Not a loud advertising surface, but a trusted bridge between personal enjoyment and the in-store expertise of established tobacconists.

Why CEDRUMO may be relevant to your shop
